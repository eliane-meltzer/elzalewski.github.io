Barter

Fall 2018 | App Concept | View full Process Book

Barter is a community trading app designed to allow people to swap items with each other. Picture Craigslist, but easier and without money.




Design Studies

Wireframes

Initial Mockups

The first challenge was figuring out how to best pair needs with excess. How will people know what items they should offer for a trade? The 'Accepting' field in each item listing allows users to specify what kinds of things they would like to receive in exchange. Each profile also has a section where users can specify what kinds of things they are typically looking for.
